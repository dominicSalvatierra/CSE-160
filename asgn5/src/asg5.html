Don't look down! My object comes up from abyss. When it crosses the 0 plane, the scene changes. To exit resulting scene and to return to the original scene, press the letter key 'O'. Not much more than that - to reenter into that second scene, the website must be reloaded.
Press 'C' to toggle between the Pointer Lock and Orbit Controls. W-A-S-D keys controls forward-left-backwards-right movements when in Pointer Lock. 'esc' can also be pressed to unlock from Pointer Controls and enable Orbit Controls.
